About Me
18+ Years of Technology Leadership & Consulting Excellence

I am a results-driven technology executive with over 18 years of experience spanning IT strategy, enterprise architecture, ERP implementations, and large-scale technology delivery across multiple continents.

My career began in software engineering at South Africa's central bank, where I designed regulatory-aligned platforms for foreign investment monitoring. I progressed through senior engineering roles at leading financial institutions — building digital banking platforms, real-time sentiment analysis tools, and mission-critical compliance systems.

I then transitioned into strategic consulting, spending a decade leading digital transformation programmes for Tier-1 clients across financial services, healthcare, mining, energy, agriculture, and telecommunications. I've led cross-functional teams of 50+ across multiple geographies, driven P&L accountability, developed go-to-market strategies, and secured multi-million-rand advisory engagements.

Today, I deliver enterprise cloud modernisation and digital transformation programmes for multinational clients, advising on GenAI adoption, cloud-native architecture, data mesh strategies, and technology modernisation at scale.

I am passionate about building high-performance teams, shaping business strategy through technology, and creating measurable impact for organisations navigating complex digital landscapes.
